Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my recent order?
You can track your recent orders perfectly by clicking the "My Orders" tab in your profile dashboard. Real-time GPS tracking is available for all dispatched items right to your doorstep.
What is the return refund timeline?
Once your returned item reaches our fulfillment center, please allow 3-5 business days for our technical team to perform a quality inspection. The refund will reflect in your original payment method within exactly 48 hours afterward.
How do I apply a Gift Card?
During the checkout phase, expand the "Apply Gift Card / Wallet" drop-down menu in your cart. Enter your 16-digit alphanumeric pin code securely. The amount will be dynamically deducted from your total payable balance instantly!
