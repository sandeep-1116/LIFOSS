Cancellation & Returns

We want you to be 100% satisfied with your purchase. Our customer-friendly policy ensures a hassle-free experience if you change your mind.

7 DAYS

Easy Returns

Don't love it? No worries. You can return most items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund. The item must be unused and in its original packaging.

10 DAYS

Free Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We offer a 10-day exchange window for fashion and lifestyle items. Our courier will pick up the old one and drop off the new one!

Instant Refunds

Once the return is picked up and verified, your refund will be initiated instantly to your LIFOSS Wallet or original payment method within 2-4 business days.

Cancellations

You can cancel your order any time before it reaches the 'Dispatched' status. Refunds for pre-paid orders will be processed immediately upon cancellation.
